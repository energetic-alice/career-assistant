**Заметки:** Mid-market Poland ML Engineer salaries reflect broad employment and B2B contract rates. Warsaw and tech hubs command 15-20% premiums; remote work equalizes regional gaps. Data reflects 2025-2026 Poland-wide sourcing, not capital-city bias alone.
