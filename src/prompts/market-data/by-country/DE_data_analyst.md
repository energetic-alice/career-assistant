**Объем вакансий:** medium
**AI-риск:** medium
**Конкуренция:** medium

**Заметки:** Mid-market data analyst salaries in Germany cluster around €50-55K entry-level and €55-74K senior, below national average of €53.9K. Big Tech and financial services (e.g., fintech in Frankfurt) can reach 15-25% above these ranges, particularly in Munich and Frankfurt. Bonuses rare in mid-market; lead roles may see €2-5K discretionary bonuses.
