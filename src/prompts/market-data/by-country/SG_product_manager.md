**Заметки:** Mid-market Product Manager salaries in Singapore range from SGD 78K-190K across career stages. Big Tech (Google, Meta, ByteDance) and pre-IPO unicorns significantly exceed these figures, reaching SGD 198K-210K+ in total compensation; FAANG offices pay approximately 25-30% premiums over mid-market baseline. Singapore's strong fintech and e-commerce sectors create intense competition for PM talent but concentrate premium pay in those verticals, not broad mid-market roles.
