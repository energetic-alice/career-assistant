**Заметки:** Mid-market recruiter salaries in Netherlands reflect national averages excluding Big Tech hubs; PayScale reports €40,971 average for technical recruiters with 1-4 years experience earning ~€38k and 5-9 years earning ~€46k. Amsterdam and specialized tech recruitment (FAANG, financial services) command 25-40% premiums above these mid-market figures-levels.fyi reports Amsterdam median €80,743 but this reflects concentrated high-tech cluster, not representative mid-market. Interpolation applied to lead tier due to sparse primary data for 8+ years experience in this country.
