### Step 4: Key Trends (2026-2027)
Search results offered general insights on EU IT/remote markets but no role-specific or 2026-2027 projections:
- Germany, Netherlands, Ireland, Poland, and Czechia rank highly for remote tech talent due to skilled engineering pools, digital infrastructure, and English proficiency.[1]
- Remote/hybrid postings remain at or near peaks in Germany (15-16%), UK, and Spain (18%), but lower in Netherlands (~7-10%) and Italy; tech sector declines impact overall remote shares.[2]
- EU IT market more balanced in 2025, with high demand from digitalization but improved talent supply (>60% of pros considering job changes).[4]
- Poland and Czechia noted for cost-effective IT talent; Ireland for tech hubs and remote policies.[1][3]
- No data on AI hiring impact, regulatory changes, or specific tech/role growth.[4][5]
